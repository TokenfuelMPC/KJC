Honest fit

Is this for you?

It is, if…

  • You're a retired, exited, or sitting leader ready to invest in your next chapter
  • You're as collaborative as you are competitive — generous and genuine
  • You value confidentiality and respect safe spaces to share
  • You love to work and love to have fun, the occasional f-bomb included
  • You already have noted success and want better PR and a story brand

It isn't, if…

  • You'd be choosing between this and covering basic needs
  • You'd rather consume passively than create and delegate
  • You have no drive to do anything differently — and you're fine settling
  • You believe you must do everything yourself and micromanage others
  • You've no interest in growing your brand, wellness, or community

The story behind the story

I had it all — except all of me.

A small-town latchkey kid turned global citizen, KJ learned the art of resiliency young. By 40 she was forced to start over, end better. Now, in her second act, she rallies high-achiever audiences toward their own next quest, after success.

"What's fun gets done. Let's walk out of the woods — together."

Before moving from entertainment to entrepreneurship, KJ acted opposite names you grew up watching, and produced viral documentaries and photography seen on Netflix, Oprah's Super Soul Sunday, O Magazine, CNN, ELLEN, ESPN, and SUCCESS. Her co-produced film, The Other Shore: The Diana Nyad Story, became the Oscar-nominated biopic NYAD, starring Annette Bening and Jodie Foster.

A #1 bestselling co-author and host of The Dreamcatchers District Podcast — with listeners in 70+ countries — KJ has captured and interviewed music legends, A-list actors, Super Bowl champions, moonshot founders, and viral TED thought leaders for two decades. Her keynotes pull from Esther Perel, Simon Sinek, Joseph Campbell, Arthur Brooks, and Jesse Itzler. Audiences leave with a laugh, a cry, and a quest into action.
